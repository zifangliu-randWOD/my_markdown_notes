# Phase 1: Positional Foundation (Weeks 1–8)

## Phase Goals

- Rebuild snatch and jerk positions through high-volume complexes at moderate loads
- Build squat volume base (sets of 5–8) to drive hypertrophy and tendon adaptation
- Begin left knee rehab — unilateral work every session
- Unlock thoracic mobility for front rack and jerk
- Establish gymnastics skill practice habits (ring MU, HS walk)

## Phase 1 Weekly Template

| Day | Primary | Secondary | Accessory (15-20 min) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Monday | Snatch complexes | Back squat (volume) | Knee rehab + thoracic mobility |
| Tuesday | Jerk from rack + Strict press | Ring MU progressions | Front rack mobility + shoulder prehab |
| Wednesday | Clean & deadlift | — | Unilateral leg work |
| Thursday | Front squat + OHS | Metcon (12-15 min) | Thoracic mobility + ankle work |
| Friday | Snatch (full lifts) + C&J | HS walk progressions | Knee rehab + hip mobility |

> [!tip] Phase 1 Coaching Cues
> - Every snatch rep: think "knuckles down, elbows high" in the pull, "punch up and press out" in the catch
> - Jerk dip: "vertical torso, heels down, elbows forward" — if knee pain appears, reduce load and add tempo
> - Film your lifts. Review between sets. One cue at a time.

## Week 1 — Introduction & Baseline

> [!info] Week 1 Targets
> Oly intensity: 70-75% | Squat intensity: 70% | Focus: establish positions, find working weights

### Monday — Snatch Complex + Back Squat

**Warm-up (10 min):** Thoracic foam roll, banded pull-aparts, PVC snatch grip behind-the-neck press, overhead squat with empty bar, snatch balance x5

**Primary: Snatch Complex**
- Complex: Hang snatch (above knee) + Snatch from floor + OHS
- 5 sets x 1 complex @ 70% (73 kg)
- Rest 2:00 between sets
- Focus: consistent pull path, patient off the floor

**Secondary: Back Squat**
- 4 x 6 @ 70% (133 kg)
- Tempo: 3 seconds down, 1 second pause at bottom, explode up
- Focus: even weight distribution left/right, vertical shins at bottom

**Accessory Window (15-20 min):**
- Peterson step-downs: 3 x 10 each leg (bodyweight or light DB)
- Spanish squat iso hold: 3 x 30 sec
- Thoracic extension over foam roller: 3 x 10 breaths

### Tuesday — Jerk + Strict Press + Ring MU Skill

**Warm-up (10 min):** Banded lat stretch, front rack stretch with barbell, thoracic rotation on wall, push press with empty bar x10, jerk footwork drills

**Primary: Jerk from Rack**
- Push jerk: 5 x 3 @ 70% (84 kg)
- Focus: vertical dip, elbows forward, fast lockout
- If knee pain in dip: slow the dip tempo to 2 sec, reduce load 5-10%

**Secondary: Strict Press**
- 4 x 6 @ moderate (start ~60 kg, adjust by feel)
- Focus: full lockout overhead, no excessive back lean

**Gymnastics Skill: Ring Muscle-Up Progressions (5-10 min)**
- Deep ring dips: 3 x 5 (slow negative)
- False grip hang: 3 x 15 sec
- Banded ring muscle-up transitions: 3 x 3

**Accessory Window (15-20 min):**
- Banded front rack stretch: 2 x 45 sec each side
- Prone Y-T-W raises: 3 x 8 each position
