# Phase 3: Peaking & Expression (Weeks 17–24)

## Phase 3 Goals

- Peak all competition lifts toward target numbers: snatch 120, C&J 140+, back squat 200+, front squat 170+
- Heavy singles and doubles — minimal volume, maximal intensity
- Reduced accessory/prehab — by now your knee should be significantly better from 16 weeks of work
- Friday becomes a simulation day — practice working up as if testing
- Metcons reduced to 1x/week — just enough to keep the engine alive
- Gymnastics: maintenance practice only — you either have ring MU and HS walk by now or you're very close

## Phase 3 Weekly Template

| Day | Primary | Secondary | Accessory (15-20 min) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Monday | Snatch (heavy singles, openers) | Back squat (heavy singles/doubles) | Light mobility only |
| Tuesday | Clean & Jerk (heavy singles) | Light strict press | Minimal accessory |
| Wednesday | Clean & deadlift | — | Unilateral maintenance |
| Thursday | Front squat (heavy doubles/singles) | Metcon (8-10 min, 1x/week) | Mobility |
| Friday | Snatch + C&J (simulation day) | — | Recovery work |

> [!tip] Phase 3 Coaching Cues
> - "Make weight" is everything now. Never chase a number at the expense of a miss. Build confidence through successful heavy lifts.
> - If you feel off on a day, cap at 90% and call it. One bad session doesn't ruin the peak — but a tweaked muscle does.
> - Sleep, nutrition, and recovery matter more now than any other phase. Prioritize them.
> - Film everything above 85%. Review between attempts.

### Friday — Simulation Day: Snatch + C&J

> [!info] Simulation Day
> Practice the test day protocol at sub-max weights. Build up in planned jumps as if testing, but cap at ~90%. This trains the process, not just the lifts.

**Warm-up:** Full test day warm-up protocol

**Snatch Simulation:**
- Build: empty → 50% → 60% → 70% → 80% → 85% → 88-90%
- 1 attempt at each weight. Move on whether you make or miss.

**Clean & Jerk Simulation:**
- Build: empty → 50% → 60% → 70% → 80% → 85% → 88-90%
- 1 attempt at each weight.

**Gymnastics: HS Walk Maintenance**
- 3 x easy distance
