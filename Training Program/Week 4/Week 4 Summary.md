## Programming Adjustments for Week 5

1. **Bracing — non-negotiable on all working sets:**
   - Back squat ≥80 kg
   - Front squat ≥75 kg
   - Clean ≥85 kg
   - Belt-less only for warm-ups and accessories
   - *Rationale: Mon + Thu of Wk 4 both ran belt-less and both went 1+ RPE point over target; combined with the right erector tonus finding, this is the corrective.*

2. **Daily lumbar maintenance protocol** (added to every warm-up):
   - 60 sec foam roll right lumbar erector (gentle, not aggressive)
   - 60 sec couch stretch each side (front-of-hip release reduces erector load all day)
   - 90/90 breathing 2 min (already in place)
   - 5 min cat-cow + child's pose + supine spinal rotation before bed on training days

3. **Pre-session fueling/hydration** (permanent change):
   - 500 ml water in the 60–90 min pre-training window
   - ~30 g carbs (banana / rice cake) 30–60 min pre-training
   - *Rationale: Friday's near-syncope likely amplified by mild hydration deficit by rep 6 of the C&J block. This also gives a small RPE buffer at heavy loads.*

4. **Heavy clean stand-up breathing protocol** (permanent change):
   - During the stand from a clean catch: controlled exhale through pursed lips, ~50% of breath out by the time you're standing
   - At top: finish exhale, take 1 quick refill breath, *then* set up for jerk
   - Own the 1-sec pause between clean and jerk; don't chain them like a workout-style C&J

5. **Squat clean structural decision — needed before Wk 5 Wednesday:**
   - (a) Clean with wider (jerk) grip at the box — eliminates hand repositioning, keeps some squat clean reps in box session
   - (b) Mandatory 4–5 squat clean singles at 90–100 kg after every box Wednesday (15–20 min add-on)
   - (c) Accept box as power-clean session, add extra squat clean set to Fri and Mon
   - *Coach's recommendation: still (b) — minimal friction, doesn't disrupt box schedule, adds ~15 quality squat clean reps per week. Same recommendation as Wk 3 summary; this needs the actual decision now.*

6. **Anterior shoulder pre-clean warm-up — NOT added.** Three clean data points = no protocol additions. Re-evaluate naturally at first 100+ kg C&J set in Wk 5.

7. **Front squat progression:** Wk 5 target 110 kg 5×5 @ RPE target 7–8 (Wk 3 was 107 kg 5×5 @ 6.5–8.5; +3 kg with belt + 90/90 protocol).

8. **Snatch working sets:** 79 kg (75%) — same as Wk 3. With the 84 kg @ RPE 7 confidence baseline, this should feel like RPE 5–6.

9. **C&J Wk 5 working weight:** 100–105 kg range. **First time C&J enters the 100+ zone in program history.** This is the natural retest for both the anterior shoulder closure and the squat clean receiving pattern. Pre-clean breathing and heavy stand-up protocol both apply from set 1.

10. **BSS Wk 5 progression target:** 3×10 each leg @ 15–17 kg, same 3-sec descending tempo. Wk 3 Sat at 15 kg was RPE 7→7.5; deload should drop the RPE — try 17 kg on set 1, hold all three sets at 17 kg if RPE ≤7.

11. **Bodyweight monitoring:** track first thing in the morning (post-pee, pre-food, naked) for the next 2 weeks. If trending below 91 kg, food intake needs attention before Phase 2 loads start climbing.
