> [!info]- C&J — Mechanism & Coaching Notes (click to expand)
>
> **Where 98 kg sits:** clean is 70%, jerk is 82%. The jerk is the harder portion at this weight. RPE target: clean 6.5, jerk 7.
>
> **Squat clean every rep:**
> Wk 4 Wed at 100 kg fresh + Wk 5 Wed at 105 kg both clean = the squat clean receiving pattern is rebuilt. 98 kg today is well within the rebuilt territory. Don't power-clean even one rep.
>
> **Heavy clean stand-up breathing — applied:**
> - At bottom of squat catch: hold brace
> - During stand: controlled exhale through pursed lips, ~50% of breath out by the time standing
> - At top: finish exhale, take 1 quick refill breath, **own the 1-sec pause**
> - Set up jerk: re-brace, dip, drive
>
> **Jerk dive-and-drive:**
> Wk 4 Fri you noted "dive and drive for jerk is sharp, and i like it a lot" — keep that. Wider stance, heels heavy through the dip, vertical drive, lock out fast.
>
> **Bar drop on the descent:**
> At 98 kg, **drop the bar from the front rack to the platform** between reps. Don't try to ride it down. Bar-lowering is the hypothesized aggravator of the Wk 3 anterior shoulder finding; the protocol from Wk 4 review is permanent.
>
> **Knee monitoring:** Wk 5 Tue jerk @ 92 kg should have been knee 5/5 with the wider stance. Today at 98 kg + from squat clean (which has historically been better for the knee than jerk-from-rack) should also be 5/5.
