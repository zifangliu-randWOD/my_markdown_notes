## Key Findings & Patterns

### 1. Tempo Work Calibration (Highest Priority)
Every tempo lift this week exceeded target RPE by 2+ points at 70%. Confirmed pattern across back squat (Mon) and front squat (Thu). **Root cause:** Tempo adds ~10-15% effective load; 70% tempo ≈ 80-85% effective intensity. **Resolution:** All Week 2 tempo work reduced to 60-65%.

### 2. Upper Body Pressing Baseline Lower Than Expected
Strict press at 60 kg was RPE 8-9 for sets of 6. Working weight is ~50 kg (45% of bench 1RM). Normal ratio, but initial programming assumed higher. **Resolution:** Week 2 strict press set at 52 kg.

### 3. Snatch Complex Work Is Achieving Its Goal
Drive position shifted higher from Monday to Friday. Power snatching at 75% indicates good hip extension and speed. The complex format (hang + floor + OHS) is successfully building positional awareness.

### 4. Jerk Mechanics — Two Development Areas Identified
- **Dip confidence:** Slow/tentative dip due to left knee apprehension. Will improve as rehab builds tendon tolerance.
- **Grip transition:** Currently using clean grip for jerks. Wider jerk grip is the target but blocked by thoracic restriction. Gradual exploration during warm-ups.

### 5. Wednesday Box Session
Off-program session is not a problem — the volume and intensity were comparable. However, unilateral accessory work was skipped. Make up this gap in Week 2 by ensuring all Wednesday accessories are completed.
